Our Services

Translation

the process of communicating and restating the meaning of a source-language text to another target-language

Transcription

the process of producing a written transcript of something such as voice or video data.

Localization

the process of adapting a product or content to a specific locale or market.

RTL/ Bidi Language Consulting

to provide consulting and guidelines in regards to Right-To-Left and BiDirectional languages.

Content Development

the process of producing content to enhance online presence or for language research development and AI systems training purposes.

Transcreation

the process of adapting a message from one language to another, while maintaining its intent, style, tone, and context.

Language data collection

the process of gathering speech data recordings for the purpose of enhancing voice recognition systems.

Localized Apps Testing

the process of functional and linguistic testing before launching localized Apps.
